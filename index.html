Unlimited math and writing tutoring

Students have 24/7 access to tutoring in these areas at no additional cost

Personal support coach

Application process

Requirements to apply

To be eligible for admissions, students must:

  • Be in their third or fourth year of high school and be at least age 15 by the start of classes

  • Have a minimum 2.5 cumulative GPA

  • Be able to commit a minimum of 7-10 hours per week (including class time)

Apply

Complete the online application. No fee required.

Access our learning platform

Eligible students will be invited to log in to Campuswire, our online learning platform used by 500,000+ students at universities throughout the nation. Students are required to have access to a personal computer, high speed internet access, and a webcam.

Sign enrollment documents

Parents/guardians must sign the program enrollment agreement.

Register for courses

Students can then reserve a spot in upcoming, available dual enrollment course(s). If applicable, any tuition fee payments should be made at this time.

Attend orientation

Students will get an introduction to the program, including how to navigate the student platform and opportunities to meet their Support Coach and new classmates.

Tuition

*For students enrolling in their first dual enrollment course through Campus, no tuition is charged. Students are currently allowed to enroll in up to two courses. First-time students are required to pay tuition for their second course. For returning students, tuition is required for all courses.

Tuition per class

1 COURSE

$499, one-time payment

2 COURSES

$998, one-time payment

Tuition for dual enrollment courses is due at the time of course registration. Students are not able to secure a spot in class until tuition is paid (either in full or, if applicable, the first installment). Tuition is 100% refundable if a student drops a course prior to the add/drop deadline.

Course materials

All textbooks and other required course materials are free to access and readily available online.

Student experience

Live classes

All classes in the Campus Scholars programs are live so that you can directly interact with professors and peers. We keep things engaging by holding lively discussions and debates, breakout sessions, forums, and more.

Tutoring

We provide students with unlimited access to 1 on 1 math and writing tutoring at no additional cost. Our tutoring services are accessible 24/7 so that you can get the academic support you need, whenever and wherever.

Office hours

Benefit from unprecedented access to professors from world-class universities and accomplished TAs through live 1:1 office hours. Faculty are available every week so you can make connections, build rapport, and deepen your knowledge.

Support coach

Before classes begin, you'll be matched with a personal success coach who will serve as your confidant, cheerleader, and advisor all the way through graduation. They'll help you set and achieve your academic and professional goals during your time at Campus.

Events

Explore your career options and learn more about the journey to get there through exclusive Campus-hosted events featuring industry experts from influential companies like Google, TikTok, and Spotify. Also enjoy game nights, trivia nights, and themed social events.
